Biography

I am currently a Postdoc Researcher in the ECE department at HKUST, supervised by Professor Jun Zhang. Previously, I obtained the Ph.D degree in the EEE department from Imperial College London in 2025, supervised by Professor Deniz Gunduz, the M.S degree in the EECS department at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor (Ph.D->M.S, U.S visa issue) in 2022, supervised by Professor Hunseok Kim and the B.S degree in the Physics department (Class of Mathematics and Physics) at Tsinghua University in 2020 with Professor Feifei Gao as my undergraduate thesis supervisor.

Research Interests

  • Fundamentals of wireless communications. MIMO-OFDM, channel coding and modulation, semantic communications, and integrated sensing and communications.

  • Machine learning for communications. data compression, image, video, and point cloud processing, 3D Gaussian Splatting, and 3D radio maps.
