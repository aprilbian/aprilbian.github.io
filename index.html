Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at HKUST, supervised by Prof. Jun Zhang (since January 2026). I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Imperial College London in January 2026, supervised by Prof. Deniz Gunduz.

Before that, I received my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, in June 2022 (admitted as a Ph.D. student and transferred to M.S. due to U.S. visa issues), supervised by Prof. Hun-seok Kim. I earned my B.S. in Physics (Class of Mathematics and Physics) from Tsinghua University in July 2020, supervised by Prof. Feifei Gao.

Research Interests

  • Fundamentals of wireless communications. Tools: MIMO-OFDM, channel coding and modulation, semantic communications, and integrated sensing and communications.

  • Machine learning for communications. Tools: data compression, image, video, and point cloud processing, 3D Gaussian Splatting, and 3D radio maps.
